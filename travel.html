THINGS TO DO

If you are from out-of-town and planning to stay the weekend, we encourage you to explore what Minnesota has to offer! All experiences have been personally vetted by Marcie and/or Brian.

Como Park Zoo & Conservatory

Our wedding will begin after the zoo closes, so we encourage you to explore the zoo beforehand. We recommend the ostriches, sloths, and giraffes.

Walker Art Center

Take a picture by the big cherry on the spoon.

Minneapolis Institute of Art

Visit the internationally-renowned art museum located behind Marcie's alma mater.

The Trylon Cinema

Marcie once got a nosebleed in this indie movie theater and had to miss the last twenty minutes of Hausu (1977).

Sever's Corn Maze & Farm Market

Transcend space and time in the corn pit.

Mall of America®

A mall that is also a theme park. Marcie recommends getting a slice of cookie cake and perusing wares at the LEGO store.

DINING

Owamni by The Sioux Chef

Polished restaurant specializing in Indigenous cuisine. Great view of the Minnesota River.

Al's Breakfast

Famously featured by Guy Fieri on Diners, Drive-Ins and Dives.

Pimento Jamaican Kitchen & Rum Bar

Authentic jerk chicken. Marcie ate here frequently when she was a student.

Milkjam Creamery

Our favorite ice cream spot in the city.
