THINGS TO DO

If you are from out-of-town and planning to stay the weekend, we encourage you to explore what Minnesota has to offer! We have personally vetted all experiences, but if you do have any complaints, please forward a message to Brian Reinken.

Como Park Zoo & Conservatory

Our wedding will begin after the zoo closes, so we encourage you to explore the zoo beforehand. We personally recommend the ostriches, the sloths, and the giraffes.

Walker Art Center

Take a picture by the big cherry on a spoon.

Minneapolis Institute of Art

Visit the internationally-renowned art museum located behind Marcie's alma mater.

The Trylon Cinema

This is an independent cinema where Marcie once got a nosebleed and had to miss the last twenty minutes of Hausu (1977). She later redemeed herself by watching Yellow Submarine (1968) with Brian and didn't bleed even once.

Sever's Corn Maze & Farm Market

You will attain enlightenment (guaranteed) in the corn pit. If, somehow, you do not attain enlightenment, we once again humbly remind you to forward your complaints to Brian Reinken.

Mall of America®

A mall that is also a theme park. Marcie recommends getting a slice of cookie cake and perusing wares at the LEGO store.
